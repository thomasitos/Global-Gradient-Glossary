This website aims to be a platform for creatives to share their favorite gradients. You can download them for Photoshop (.grd), Gimp (.grr), or just grab the CSS code. To contribute, you can submit your gradients the old-fashioned way — details below.


THE PICTURES

The images on the left give you a preview of how each gradient looks when applied as a gradient map. The effect is achieved using SVG filters. Some great resources to understand this effect are this article by Sara Soueidan and the SVG Gradient Map Filter tool by Yoksel.


The pictures by Elaine Gay Jarvis show the 1978 Gay Freedom Day Parade in San Francisco, California. They are provided by the GLBT Historical Society on DIVA.


HOW TO SUBMIT

You're welcome to contribute your own gradients to the collection! Just email christopher@thomas-design.de with your gradient in .grd, .grr, or CSS format, along with a name for your gradients and your own name.
